Rules

General Rules and Regulations.

  • All the participants should bring their college Identity Card.
  • The date and time of arrival and departure should be notified by the participants in the registration form.
  • All the participants should bring No Objection Certificate from Director/Principle of the concerned college. Event wise list of participants may be prepared for this purpose.
  • One player can participate in maximum 2 events. Those who are participating in more than 1 event, shall need to register twice.
  • Only one team from a college will be allowed to participate in a particular event.
  • Participants of all events should report at the assigned venue at least 30 minutes before start of the respective events.
  • Two office bearers, 1M/1F, should accompany the squad from each participating college who will be responsible for discipline of their team. Officials should also bring their college Identity Card.
  • If any participant is found involved in any indisciplinary activities he/she will be debarred from the tournament immediately.
  • Illegal participation of any player/Team in any event will be responsible for disqualification of all teams of the concerned college from all events of the tournament and the college authority will be responsible for this act.
  • Religion based performances will not be allowed. Such performances may cause disqualification.
  • Decision of the judges/umpires will be final and binding to all.
  • Only winners of each event will be eligible to participate in State Level events.

Battle of Bands (Band-Wars)

Rules and Regulation:

  • It will be a Group event. Team can comprise of 4-6 members.
  • Participants can sing on the song of their own choice.
  • Duration: 10-12 Minutes
  • Participants should bring their own musical instruments.
  • The Judges decision will be final and binding to all.

Skit/Play

Rules and Regulation:

  • It will be a Group event.
  • Team can comprise of 8 - 10 members.
  • Duration: 10 Minutes.
  • The Judges decision will be final and binding to all.

Fashion Jalwa

Rules and Regulation:

  • Team can comprise of 2 members (1M/1F).
  • Materials required for the contest must be brought by the contestants themselves.
  • Detailed rules will be announced prior to each round.
  • The Judges decision will be final and binding to all.

Mimicry/Standup Comedy

Rules and Regulation:

Singing

Rules and Regulation:
