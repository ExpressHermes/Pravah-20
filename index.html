About Us

Let's flow in the PRAVAH of the exuberance of Uttar Pradesh and celebrate the carnival of AKTU's Arts and Cultural fest happening in Institute of Engineering and Technology, Lucknow. Come out and dive into the journey and experience a thrilling contest of the best talents from the state and encounter an amalgamation of the culture of Uttar Pradesh.

Pravah encompasses lavish history, splendid culture and a profoundly rich heritage of Uttar Pradesh.

The dates being 28th-29th Feb 2020, let us all plunge into a world made of the mystique of Uttar Pradesh and its budding talent. The team here at Institute of Engineering and Technology has strived hard to make Pravah a brilliant masterpiece of grandeur and transcendence.

Uttar Pradesh

The state of Uttar Pradesh, home to the largest cultural diversity and vivid variations is divided into 8 zonal centre as follows. These zonal center are based on equivalent distribution of resources and dwellers.

  • Agra
  • Prayagraj
  • Bareilly
  • Gautam Buddh Nagar
  • Ghaziabad
  • Gorakhpur
  • Lucknow
  • Meerut

Historians say, the most beautiful thing on the face of earth is The Taj. And Agra, the city home to the only wonder of the world in South Asia is one such spectacle. Also, the great Mughal Agra Fort speaks great volume of the valour of the city. Lastly, the sweet luscious Agra ka Petha adds to the treat. Districts under Agra zonal centre are Agra, Aligarh, Mathura, Hathras.
Host: Hindustan College of Science & Technology, Mathura (064)

The city of Triveni Sangam, a site wherein, three of the purest Indian rivers meet and give birth to cultural heritage and history of the highest finesse. Also housing the residence of Mr.Jawahar Lal Nehru, the vintage Anand Bhawan, the city of Allahabad is a location for all to enchant and enlive. Districts under Allahabad, Jaunpur, Mirzapur, Bhadohi, Varansi.
Host: Kashi Institute of Technology, Varanasi(428)

The town of Bareilly, latest inclusion in the NCR region is a centre of multi-dimensional trades and food industry. Famous for four Shiva temples in four corners of the region, the city is also called by the name Fairy. Bareilly also boasts of a number of intense military installations. Districts under Bareilly, Moradabad, Shikohabad, Sitapur.
Host: Shri Ram Murti Smarak College of Engg & Technology, Bareilly (014)

A largely sub-urban district in the Northern parts of Uttar Pradesh, Gautam Buddh Nagar has evolved as a centre for technical education and industrial development. Greater Noida and Noida are some well-planned cities and the region as a whole has gone through rapid urbanization, making it the elite among all in Uttar Pradesh. District under Noida / Greater Noida (G.B. Nagar).
Host: I.T.S. Engineering College Greater Noida (222)

Referred to as the 'Gateway to UP', it is a large and efficiently planned industrial city. Adorned by swanky apartment high-rises and beautifully structured highways, Ghaziabad adds heavily to the legend of modern Uttar Pradesh. Yet the history of the city remains preserved in the lanes of old Ghaziabad, the original one. Districts under Ghaziabad, Modinagar.
Host: ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad (032)

Located in close proximity to the republic of Nepal, the city is home to the famous Gorakhnath Temple. The city of Gorakhpur also houses the Geeta Press, publisher of religious scripts for decades. Additionally, the city takes pride in the numerous poets and artists it has produced over the years.e history of the city remains preserved in the lanes of old Ghaziabad, the original one. Districts under Gorakhpur, Gonda, Mahrajganj, Basti, Azamgarh, Ballia, Ghazipur.
Host: KIPM College of Engineering & Technology, Gorakhpur (751)

The only city in the state to boast of a functional metro-rail system. The capital of Uttar Pradesh is no less when it comes to vivid portrayal of history, culture and modern leisure. The Chikankari, the Nawabi cuisine the mesmerising parks and the Lucknawi accent, all goes into making the present-day Awadh a must have on your checklists. Districts under Lucknow, Raebareli, Barabanki, Sultanpur, Amethi, Ambedkar Nagar, Kanpur, Unnao, Hardoi.
Host: Maharana Pratap Engineering College, Kanpur (046)

Mentioned often as 'the sports city of India', this jewel in the crown of UP sits right at the top. The city that played host to the first war of independence still breathes fire when it comes to patriotism. Also, popular for various sporting equipments and talents budding here, Meerut makes UP prouder all the more. Districts under Meerut, Bagpat, Bijnore, Saharanpur, Shamli, Muzaffar Nagar.
Host: Vidya College of Engineering, Meerut (229)
