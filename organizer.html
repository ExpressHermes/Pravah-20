About IET

Planted three decades ago, a seed in the high gardens of lucknow to provide the shade of knowledge, it is enriching its pupils since then. It took roots in the year which was marked with milestones such as birth of the internet entrepreneur Mark Zuckerberg and initiation of sale of Macintosh in the US. Established in 1984, it marked the genesis of an academia, the pursuit of research, education, and scholarship and thus the odyssey had begun. Growing from strength to strength and being recognised as inimitable college of North India, the institute today is the leading establishment in the field of education and research. The Institute is redeemed with the best scholars and professors of the state, acquiring numerous laurels under it's name since its enactment.
